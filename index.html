About

A lung cancer predicting app that uses machine learning would utilize algorithms and statistical models to analyze data from symptoms inputted by the user to predict the likelihood of lung cancer.

How it works

This app require the user to input various symptoms, such as coughing, wheezing, shortness of breath, and chest pain. The machine learning algorithms would then analyze this data and provide a prediction of the likelihood of lung cancer. The accuracy of the prediction depends on data inputted. Overall, such an app could potentially be a valuable tool for early detection and prevention of lung cancer.
